Built for the Athletic Nation.

South Africa's all-in-one running platform designed to unite runners, clubs, and federations through smart, seamless technology.

Our Mission

To empower the South African running community by making it easier to find events, join clubs, track achievements, and manage athletics from the grassroots to the national level in an all in one app.

What is GoRunSA?

GoRunSA is a mobile and web platform for runners, clubs, schools, educational institutions, federations, scouts, and sponsors. Users can track their runs, sync with devices, join and manage clubs, participate in events, view leaderboards, access AI-powered training tools, and connect with sponsors all in one place. Scouts can discover and track emerging talent, while sponsors can engage with the running community.

Who Uses GoRunSA?

  • 🏃 Individual runners and athletes
  • 👟 Running clubs and club administrators
  • 🏫 Schools, universities, and sports institutions
  • 📋 ASA officials and talent scouts
  • 🤝 Sponsors and brand partners
  • 📋 National and regional federations

Why Garmin Integration?

  • 🔁 Seamless syncing of Garmin device activities to GoRunSA profiles
  • 📋 Automatic updates to training logs, achievements, and leaderboards
  • 📊 Deeper analytics and personalized insights for athletes and coaches
  • 👥 Enhanced event participation tracking for clubs and federations
  • 📶 Access real time activity data for member and club analytics
  • 🤖 Enable smart AI coaching based on Garmin Stats and for athletes coaches
