🏃‍♂️ About Us 📘

GoRunSA is a proudly South African sport-tech platform created to unite and uplift the country’s running community. Whether you're an elite athlete, a weekend runner, a school coach, or a club administrator, GoRunSA is built with you in mind.

We simplify how clubs manage memberships, how athletes get discovered, and how communities stay connected through the power of running. By combining technology with local insight, we’re building something far more than just an app we’re building a digital home for South African athletics.

GoRunSA was founded with a personal mission: to eliminate the barriers that stop young athletes from being seen and supported, and to reduce the overwhelming admin load placed on clubs and volunteers. With a growing passion for technology and sport, we’re creating tools that make running more accessible, rewarding, and impactful for all.

🎯 Our Mission 🏁

To connect, empower, and grow South Africa’s running communities through smart, accessible technology.

  • 💻 Digitize grassroots athletics with tools that reduce admin and improve access.
  • 🏅 Give every runner a fair chance to shine—regardless of background or location.
  • 🤝 Support clubs and coaches with modern tools for management, communication, and growth.
  • ❤️ Promote health, opportunity, and unity through the culture of running.

🔭 Our Vision 🌄

To become South Africa’s leading athletics platform trusted by clubs, runners, schools, and federations to drive performance, discovery, and community impact.

💡 What We Believe 🌱

  • 🌍 Running is for everyone. Talent exists everywhere. Opportunity should too.
  • 🛠️ Technology should empower, not replace. We build tools that make clubs and coaches stronger not sidelined.
  • 🤝 Community is everything. Behind every great runner is a great support system.
